About

PathMe is developed and maintained in an academic capacity by Daniel Domingo-Fernández at the Fraunhofer SCAI Department of Bioinformatics.

References

Resources

Resources from KEGG, Reactome, WikiPathways, and MSigDB have been used to build this package and application.

KEGG

License

KEGG KGML files are downloaded via the KEGG API for academic purposes (Terms and conditions). More info at Bio2BEL KEGG.

  • Kanehisa, M. and Goto, S.; KEGG: Kyoto Encyclopedia of Genes and Genomes. Nucleic Acids Res. 28, 27-30 (2000).
